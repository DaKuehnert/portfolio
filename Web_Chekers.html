  • Extras:

    Taking a jump before and after

    Pieces can jump over an imediate diagnol piece. Once the turn is submitted, the piece jumped over will be removed.

    • Details

    Forced jumps

    If atleast one capture jump is available, the player is restricted to taking one of those possible jumps.

    • Details

    Multiple jump move

    A piece may make multiple captures in a single turn by jumping over pieces consecutively.

    • Details

    Kinging a piece

    Upon reaching the king row, a simple piece will turn into a king after the turn submission.

    • Details

    Resigning from a game

    At any point, a player can choose to resign from the game (or sign out). This will put the game into an end state, where a message will be displayed to show who’s the winner and loser/resignee.

    • Details

    Game Victory

    When all of an opponent’s pieces are captured, the capturing player will win. The game will then enter an end of game state, announcing the winner and loser. Players can exit the game by clicking the ‘Exit’ button

    • Details

    Game Invite

    Users can chose to accept or reject a game invitation. Allows players to avoid playing with losers who have no friends

    • Details

    Spectator Mode

    Users logged onto the server can view ongoing games. This allows for lonely users to watch other users have more fun than them

    • Details

    AI

    Players can chose to compete between two differnt level of difficulties, Basic AI, Advanced AI. The goal is to relieve lonliness for those with no friends by providing them with someone to play with.

    This is not an actual AI, here A.I. stands for Algorithmic Ivan. The name we gave for the max min pruning algorithm used to produce the robot player.
